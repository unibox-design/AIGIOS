## QA

Here are the top 5 risks with mitigations for the proposed autonomous PR-only delivery plan:

1. Risk: Misconfigured or Bypassed Branch Protection Rules  
   Mitigation:  
   - Regularly audit and enforce branch protection settings.  
   - Limit admin privileges that allow bypassing protections.  
   - Set up alerts for any direct pushes or unauthorized merges.

2. Risk: Gaps or Failures in CI Pipeline Quality and Coverage  
   Mitigation:  
   - Maintain broad and deep test coverage including linting, unit, integration, and security tests.  
   - Monitor and fix flaky tests promptly.  
   - Enforce blocking merges on any failing CI jobs.

3. Risk: Manual Approval Process Causing Delays or Mistakes  
   Mitigation:  
   - Define clear approval SLAs and roles.  
   - Train approvers thoroughly on criteria and risks.  
   - Use reminders and automated escalation for pending approvals.

4. Risk: Delayed or Missed Failure Detection in Deployment Monitoring  
   Mitigation:  
   - Integrate reliable monitoring systems with alerting tightly coupled to workflows.  
   - Regularly test alert triggers and notification workflows.  
   - Document procedures for incident handling upon alerts.

5. Risk: Rollback Mechanism Failures or Unpreparedness  
   Mitigation:  
   - Automate rollback triggers and thoroughly test rollback workflows.  
   - Keep artifacts well versioned and tagged.  
   - Conduct regular rollback drills and maintain clear rollback documentation.
